Why choose an individual/freelancer?


Personal interaction: You are directly talking to the programmer & designer

Clear communication: Your project details are understood by a single person

Cost effective: Working with individuals costs less


And why choose me, specifically?


Peronalized website: I build without templates. Your website can be exactly what you want it to be

Lightweight website: I use what is called JAMstack, a style of website architecture that delivers better performance, higher security, lower cost of scaling

Low maintainence cost: The architecture I use is also hyper-static i.e. it has all the interactivity of dynamic websites without the need for server-side programming. This brings down the annual cost of hosting your website by a big margin

Reliable timeline: I hold myself accountable to a clear timeline. See more on the page about process
